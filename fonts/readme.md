# Font folder structure.

Fonts are served from within their own folder, named after the fonts within.
e.g. for meta: 

    fonts/
    +-- metaweb/
    |   _import.scss
    |   MetaWeb-Bold.eot
    |   MetaWeb-Bold.woff
    |   MetaWeb-Bolditalic.eot
    |   MetaWeb-Bolditalic.woff
    |   MetaWeb-Light.eot
    |   MetaWeb-Light.woff
    |   MetaWeb-Medium.eot
    |   MetaWeb-Medium.woff
    |   MetaWeb-Normal.eot
    |   MetaWeb-Normal.woff
    |   MetaWeb-Normalitalic.eot
    |   MetaWeb-Normalitalic.woff
